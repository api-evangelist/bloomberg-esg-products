# Bloomberg ESG Products

Bloomberg ESG Products provide environmental, social, and governance data, analytics, and scores to help investors assess sustainability risks and opportunities. Bloomberg collects ESG data from thousands of companies globally, offering ESG scores, climate data, green bond data, and sustainable finance analytics.

## APIs

- **Bloomberg ESG Data API** - ESG scores and company sustainability metrics
- **Bloomberg Climate Data API** - Physical and transition climate risk data
- **Bloomberg Green Bond Data** - Green, social, and sustainability bond data
